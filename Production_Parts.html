Webginet: Precision Production Parts for Modern Manufacturing

Webginet delivers high-quality, precision-engineered production parts tailored to your manufacturing needs. We support OEMs, industrial operations, and automation providers with reliable components, custom fabrication, and streamlined part supply chains.

From small-batch prototypes to high-volume production runs — if it keeps your machines moving or your products complete, Webginet makes it, sources it, and delivers it on time.

CNC Machined Components

Webginet specializes in CNC-machined production parts made from aluminum, steel, brass, and polymers. With tolerances down to microns, we deliver precision parts for critical assemblies in automation, automotive, and aerospace.

Custom Fabrication & Assembly

Need one-of-a-kind components or specialized fixtures? Our fabrication shop builds custom production parts, subassemblies, jigs, and machine accessories to exact specifications with quality assurance built in.

Fasteners & Mechanical Components

Webginet offers a vast catalog of standard and specialty fasteners, bearings, seals, gaskets, bushings, and other critical mechanical parts — available on-demand for your production floor.

Vendor-Managed Inventory (VMI)

We keep your production line running with replenishment-ready inventory services. Webginet's VMI programs ensure critical production parts are stocked, tracked, and available exactly when and where needed.
