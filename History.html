Webginet History

From a small team of dedicated electricians in Dallas to a nationally recognized name in smart electrical solutions — this is the story of Webginet. A journey of resilience, innovation, and unmatched service spanning over a decade.

Our Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2010, Webginet Electricians started as a small, family-run electrical service provider in Dallas, Texas. With a vision to revolutionize the electrical industry, we focused on integrity, excellence, and forward-thinking service.

From residential wiring jobs to our first industrial project, every step laid the foundation for what we are today — a trusted name in digital and industrial transformation across the U.S.

Scaling with Purpose

Over the past decade, we’ve evolved from electrical contractors to strategic partners in smart technology, automation, and sustainable infrastructure. Our expansion into commercial and industrial sectors was driven by our passion to deliver future-ready solutions.

With 350+ in-house electricians and over 1,200 successful projects, Webginet is recognized for consistency, quality, and innovation.
